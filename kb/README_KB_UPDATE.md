## Key Business Rules
- Push packages first.
- Mention individual services only when relevant.
- All prices are in AUD.
- Instalments are available with an additional AUD 10 charge.
- Urgent delivery must be referred to the team because it depends on availability.
- DreamShift guarantees interviews, not jobs.
- Interview preparation is a guide, not a guarantee of interview performance.
- Essential revisions: unlimited within 1 week.
- Advanced/Ultimate revisions: unlimited within 1 month.
