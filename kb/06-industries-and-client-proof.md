# Industries & Client Proof

## Main Proof Points
DreamShift may mention:
- 100+ clients supported to land their dream job in Australia
- 91% landed interviews after obtaining DreamShift services
- 100+ client reviews across Trustpilot, Google, and WhatsApp
- 40+ industries supported

## Careful Wording
Use safe wording such as:
- DreamShift has supported 100+ clients.
- 91% of clients landed interviews after obtaining DreamShift services.
- Results vary based on industry, experience, visa status, and job market conditions.
- Terms apply to the 60-day guarantee.

Avoid saying:
- Every client gets the same result.
- Every industry has the same outcome.
- DreamShift guarantees jobs.
- DreamShift guarantees hiring decisions.
