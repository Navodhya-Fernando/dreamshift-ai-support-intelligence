# Sales Objections & Safe Replies

## Source
The following key objections were confirmed from the DreamShift sales team:
- Price
- Timing
- Not in Australia at the moment
- Rescheduling the consultation call if missed
- Not applying on behalf of the client / user wants job application support

The chatbot should prioritize these objections when answering sales-related questions.

## Objection: Price
User may say:
- The package is expensive.
- AUD 750 / AUD 800 / AUD 1500 is too much.
- I only need a CV.
- I cannot pay everything at once.

Safe reply:
I understand. DreamShift packages are not just a CV rewrite. They include a wider job-search toolkit such as job market research, ATS keyword research, Resume/CV writing, Cover Letter writing, LinkedIn optimization, strategy, and support depending on the package. You also do not have to pay everything at once. Instalment options are available with an additional AUD 10 charge, and the exact arrangement can be discussed during the free consultation call before you decide whether to proceed.

Recommended action:
- Push package comparison first.
- Mention individual services only if the user asks or seems price-sensitive.
- If price remains the main concern, guide the user to a free consultation.

Individual service fallback:
- Resume/CV Writing: AUD 400 onwards
- Cover Letter Writing: AUD 150 onwards
- LinkedIn Optimization: AUD 350 onwards

## Objection: Timing
User may say:
- How fast can you deliver?
- I need this urgently.
- Can you deliver before a specific deadline?
- I have a job closing soon.

Safe reply:
Urgent delivery may be possible depending on team availability. It is best to contact DreamShift or book a free consultation so the team can check your deadline and confirm whether it can be accommodated.

Important rule:
The chatbot must not promise urgent delivery, same-day delivery, 24-hour delivery, 48-hour delivery, or any fixed expedited fee unless the team has confirmed availability.

## Objection: Not in Australia at the moment
User may say:
- I am not in Australia yet.
- I am overseas but targeting Australian jobs.
- I am planning to move to Australia.
- Can you still help me?

Safe reply:
Yes, DreamShift can support clients who are targeting the Australian job market even if they are not currently in Australia. The team can help position your Resume/CV, Cover Letter, and LinkedIn for relevant Australian roles based on your background, target roles, and work rights. However, DreamShift does not provide migration or visa advice.

Recommended action:
Ask:
- Are you targeting jobs in Australia?
- What roles or industries are you targeting?
- What is your current visa/work rights situation, if you are comfortable sharing?
- Are you already applying, or preparing before arrival?

Important rule:
Do not give migration, visa, PR, or legal advice. Recommend speaking to a registered migration professional for visa-specific guidance.

## Objection: Rescheduling the call if missed
User may say:
- I missed my consultation call.
- Can I reschedule?
- I could not attend the call.
- I booked a call but missed it.

Safe reply:
Yes, you can reschedule the consultation. Please use the booking link or contact DreamShift so the team can arrange another available time.

Recommended action:
- Keep the response friendly and low-pressure.
- Encourage the user to rebook.
- Do not make the user feel penalized.
- Do not promise a specific time slot unless booking availability is known.

## Objection: DreamShift is not applying on behalf of me / I want job application support
User may say:
- Can you apply for jobs for me?
- I want you to handle applications.
- I do not have time to apply.
- I want job application support.
- I thought DreamShift applies on my behalf.

Safe reply:
Job application support is available in the Ultimate Career Package, which includes 2 months of job application support along with a dedicated senior writer and end-to-end support. The Essential and Advanced packages focus on building your job-search toolkit, documents, LinkedIn, strategy, and guidance, but they do not include the same level of application support as Ultimate.

Recommended action:
- If the user wants DreamShift to handle applications, recommend the Ultimate Career Package.
- If the user is unsure, suggest a free consultation so the team can explain exactly what job application support includes.
- Do not claim that all packages include applying on behalf of the client.

Important rule:
The chatbot must not say DreamShift applies to unlimited jobs.
The chatbot must not promise that job application support guarantees interviews or jobs.
The chatbot must clearly separate job application support from interview/job outcome guarantees.

## Objection: Can you guarantee a job?
Safe reply:
DreamShift guarantees interviews, not job offers. Job offers depend on interview performance, employer decisions, experience, visa eligibility, market demand, and other factors. DreamShift helps improve your interview chances through stronger documents, LinkedIn positioning, job market research, ATS keyword alignment, and job search strategy.

## Objection: How does the 60-day interview guarantee work?
Safe reply:
If you do not land at least one interview within 60 days of receiving finalized documents, and you meet the guarantee conditions, you can request either a 50% refund or a complete rewrite of your CV, Cover Letter, and LinkedIn at no additional cost.

## Objection: I do not have Australian experience
Safe reply:
Not having Australian experience can be challenging, but it does not mean your background has no value. DreamShift focuses on identifying transferable skills, relevant achievements, projects, and market-aligned keywords so your experience is communicated clearly for Australian recruiters.

## Objection: I already applied to many jobs and got no interviews
Safe reply:
That usually means the issue may be role targeting, ATS keywords, CV structure, LinkedIn positioning, or how your achievements are communicated. DreamShift starts with job market research and ATS keyword research before writing, so the documents are built around the roles you are targeting.

## Objection: How do I know this is not generic or AI-written?
Safe reply:
DreamShift uses a structured process that includes job market research, ATS keyword research, client questionnaires, existing document review, and specialist input. The aim is to build documents around your actual background and target roles, not generic templates.
