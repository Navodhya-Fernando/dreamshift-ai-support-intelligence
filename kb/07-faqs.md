# DreamShift FAQs

## Do you guarantee a job?
No. DreamShift guarantees interviews, not job offers. The 60-day guarantee means that if you do not land at least one interview within 60 days of receiving finalized documents, and you meet the guarantee conditions, you may request either a 50% refund or a complete rewrite of your documents.

## How does the 60-day guarantee work?
The guarantee begins from the date finalized documents are delivered. To qualify, you must actively apply to relevant roles using DreamShift documents, the roles must match your field, experience level, and visa eligibility, and the claim must be submitted within 75 days.

## Can I pay in instalments?
Yes. Instalments are available with an additional AUD 10 charge. The exact payment arrangement can be discussed during the free consultation call, and you can decide after the call whether you want to proceed.

## Do I have to pay everything at once?
No. You do not have to pay everything at once. Instalment options are available with an additional AUD 10 charge.

## Do you offer individual services?
Yes. Individual services are available:
- Resume/CV Writing: AUD 400 onwards
- Cover Letter Writing: AUD 150 onwards
- LinkedIn Optimization: AUD 350 onwards

However, most clients choose packages because they include the full job-search toolkit.

## Which package should I choose?
Essential is best if you have one specific job opening. Advanced is best if you want experts to handle every document and guide you. Ultimate is best if you want DreamShift to apply for jobs and handle everything for you.

## Do you support urgent deadlines?
Urgent delivery may be possible depending on team availability. Contact DreamShift or book a free consultation so the team can check your deadline and confirm whether it can be accommodated.

## What is the turnaround time?
Turnaround time can vary depending on the package, client responsiveness, and team availability. The website may indicate that completed documents are typically delivered within 5–14 days, but urgent timelines should be confirmed directly with the team.

## Do you offer revisions?
Yes. Revision periods depend on the package:
- Essential: unlimited revisions within 1 week
- Advanced: unlimited revisions within 1 month
- Ultimate: unlimited revisions within 1 month

## What if I only need a CV?
Individual Resume/CV Writing is available from AUD 400 onwards. However, if you are actively job searching in Australia, a package may be more effective because it can include ATS keyword research, cover letters, LinkedIn optimization, and support.

## Do you work with my industry?
DreamShift has worked with clients across 40+ industries, including finance, IT, education, government, HR, logistics, manufacturing, retail, sales, marketing, telecommunications, hospitality, and more. If your industry is not listed, book a free consultation so the team can check your background.

## Do you help 485 visa holders?
DreamShift can help position your Resume/CV, Cover Letter, and LinkedIn for relevant Australian roles based on your experience, field, and work rights. However, visa or migration advice should be discussed with a registered migration professional.

## Do you use AI to write CVs?
DreamShift’s process includes job market research, ATS keyword research, client questionnaires, specialist writers, and document strategy. The chatbot should not claim the work is purely AI-written. The safe answer is that DreamShift uses a structured human-led process to create tailored documents.
