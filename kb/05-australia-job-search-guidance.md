# Australia Job Search Guidance

## Purpose
This file gives the chatbot Australia-focused career context. It should help explain why DreamShift packages are built around research, ATS keywords, LinkedIn, and job search strategy.

## Common Australia Job Search Challenges
Australian job seekers, especially migrants, graduates, and temporary visa holders, may struggle with:
- Not knowing which roles to target
- Not understanding local job market expectations
- Using generic CVs across multiple roles
- Not matching ATS keywords
- Weak LinkedIn positioning
- Lack of Australian experience
- Weak networking or internal referral strategy
- Applying to many jobs without interviews
- Unclear visa or work rights messaging

## How DreamShift Helps
DreamShift helps by:
- Researching the target job market
- Identifying relevant job titles
- Mapping skills and keywords from job descriptions
- Creating targeted Resume/CV versions
- Creating targeted Cover Letter versions
- Optimizing LinkedIn for recruiter visibility
- Providing job search strategy
- Providing guides for outreach and referrals
- Providing interview preparation guides for interviews

## Temporary Visa / 485 Visa Users
The chatbot must not provide migration advice.

Safe wording:
DreamShift can help position your Resume/CV, Cover Letter, and LinkedIn for relevant Australian roles based on your experience, field, and work rights. However, visa or migration advice should be discussed with a registered migration professional.

## No Australian Experience
Safe wording:
Not having Australian experience can be challenging, but your international experience, projects, transferable skills, and achievements can still be positioned strongly. DreamShift focuses on aligning your profile with what Australian recruiters are looking for, while keeping everything honest and evidence-based.

## Applied to Many Jobs But No Interviews
Safe wording:
If you have applied to many jobs and received no interviews, the issue may be your targeting, ATS keyword alignment, Resume/CV structure, LinkedIn positioning, or how your experience is being communicated. DreamShift reviews the job market first, then builds documents around relevant roles rather than using a generic CV.

## Internal Referrals
DreamShift may provide message templates and guidance on reaching out to connections for internal referrals as part of the broader job search toolkit.
