# DreamShift Packages & Pricing

## Pricing Currency
All package prices are in AUD.

## Payment Note
Clients do not have to pay everything at once. Instalment options are available with an additional AUD 10 charge. The exact payment arrangement can be discussed during the free consultation call, and the client can decide after the call whether they want to proceed.

## Essential Package
Price: AUD 750

Payment note: You do not have to pay at once.

Ideal for: Someone who has one specific job opening to apply for.

Includes:
- Resume/CV Writing: 1 version
- Cover Letter Writing: 1 version
- LinkedIn Optimization: Basic
- ATS Keyword Research
- Consultation: 2 calls
- Ongoing Support: 1 month

Revision policy:
- Unlimited revisions within 1 week after initial draft/final package delivery stage, depending on project flow.

Best recommendation logic:
Recommend Essential when the user:
- Has one specific job opening.
- Needs one Resume/CV and one Cover Letter.
- Wants a more focused service.
- Does not need multiple document versions.
- Does not need job application support.
- Is more budget-conscious but still wants a full professional package.

## Advanced Package
Price: AUD 800

Payment note: You do not have to pay at once.

Ideal for: Someone who wants experts to handle every document and guide them through the job search.

Includes:
- Resume/CV Writing: 3–4 versions
- Cover Letter Writing: 3–4 versions
- LinkedIn Optimization: Advanced
- LinkedIn Banner + Free Designs
- ATS Keyword Research
- Consultation: Unlimited calls
- Job Search Strategy: 50+ jobs in 10 hours
- Interview Preparation Guide: for each interview
- Ongoing Support: until the client lands a job

Revision policy:
- Unlimited revisions within 1 month.

Best recommendation logic:
Recommend Advanced when the user:
- Is actively job searching in Australia.
- Is targeting multiple role types.
- Needs multiple Resume/CV and Cover Letter versions.
- Wants LinkedIn optimization and branding.
- Wants expert guidance throughout the process.
- Wants job search strategy but does not need DreamShift to apply for jobs on their behalf.
- Wants stronger support than Essential.

## How to Recommend Packages
If the user asks which package is best, ask:
1. Are you applying for one specific job, or multiple roles?
2. Do you need LinkedIn optimization as well?
3. Do you want DreamShift to guide you, or also apply for jobs for you?
4. How soon do you need the documents?
5. Are you mainly targeting the Australian job market?

Then recommend:
- Essential for one specific job opening.
- Advanced for most active Australian job seekers needing multiple versions and full guidance.
- Ultimate for users who want job application support and end-to-end handling.

## Individual Service Fallback
If the user says they only need one service, mention:
- Resume/CV Writing: AUD 400 onwards
- Cover Letter Writing: AUD 150 onwards
- LinkedIn Optimization: AUD 350 onwards

Then ask whether they want to compare individual services or packages.
