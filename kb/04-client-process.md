# DreamShift Client Process

## Overview
DreamShift offers an all-in-one job toolkit. This means everything the client needs for their job search is organized in one place, including:
- Resume/CV versions
- Cover letter versions
- LinkedIn optimization
- Job market research
- ATS keyword report
- Job search guides
- Interview preparation guides
- Optional job application support depending on package

## Step 1: WhatsApp Group Setup
After onboarding, DreamShift adds the client to a WhatsApp group with the relevant team members.

## Step 2: Existing Documents and Career Direction
DreamShift asks the client to send existing documents and answer questions about:
- Jobs they want to apply for
- Target countries and locations
- Target industries and sectors
- Career direction
- Experience and goals

## Step 3: Job Market Research
DreamShift’s job market analysts conduct in-depth research on the target job market.

The job search report may include:
- Jobs the client can apply for
- Similar job titles
- Market expectations
- Role direction

The purpose is not only for the client to apply to those jobs, but also for DreamShift to understand the market deeply before writing the documents.

## Step 4: ATS Keyword / Job Market Report
DreamShift creates an ATS keyword report or job market report.

For each target job title, the report may include:
- Similar job titles
- Areas of expertise required
- Soft skills
- Technical skills
- Qualifications
- Recruiter expectations

This helps both the client and DreamShift understand what recruiters are looking for.

## Step 5: Questionnaire
DreamShift sends the client a questionnaire. This works like a written one-on-one interview.

The questionnaire helps capture:
- Key projects
- Problems solved
- Achievements
- Work beyond normal duties
- International clients
- Transferable skills
- Career strengths
- Evidence that makes the client stand out

## Step 6: Resume/CV Writing
The CV writer reviews:
- Existing documents
- Questionnaire responses
- ATS reports
- Job market research
- Target roles

Then DreamShift writes the Resume/CV in a fully editable Word document.

Depending on package, the client may receive:
- Essential: 1 Resume/CV version
- Advanced: 3–4 Resume/CV versions
- Ultimate: 3–4 Resume/CV versions

## Step 7: Cover Letter Writing
DreamShift creates cover letters aligned with the Resume/CV versions.

Depending on package:
- Essential: 1 Cover Letter version
- Advanced: 3–4 Cover Letter versions
- Ultimate: 3–4 Cover Letter versions

## Step 8: LinkedIn Optimization
After the CV project, the LinkedIn specialist takes over.

DreamShift prepares LinkedIn content, often in a Google Doc, before updating or guiding updates.

LinkedIn optimization focuses on:
- Headline
- Skills and keywords
- Top five skills
- Other skills sections
- Experience bullet points
- Project section
- Contributor/project credibility
- Recommendations guidance
- Recruiter-friendly profile structure

## Step 9: LinkedIn Branding
DreamShift also focuses on LinkedIn branding.

This may include:
- Personalized LinkedIn banner image
- Featured section images
- Visuals that communicate the client’s specialty
- Personal brand design direction

## Step 10: Job Search Guides
DreamShift provides additional guides such as:
- Recruiting firm lists
- Message templates
- Networking outreach templates
- Internal referral guidance

## Step 11: Interview Preparation Guides
Advanced and Ultimate packages include interview preparation guides for each interview.

Important: These are guides. DreamShift does not guarantee job offers or interview performance.

## Step 12: Ongoing Support
Ongoing support depends on package:
- Essential: 1 month
- Advanced: until the client lands a job
- Ultimate: until the client lands a job

Ultimate also includes 2 months of job application support.
