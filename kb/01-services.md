# DreamShift Services

## Main Service Direction
DreamShift should primarily guide users toward packages because packages include the full job-search toolkit and provide better support for Australian job seekers.

If a user only wants one specific service, individual services are available.

## Package-Based Services
DreamShift packages may include:
- Resume/CV Writing
- Cover Letter Writing
- LinkedIn Optimization
- LinkedIn Banner and Free Designs
- ATS Keyword Research
- Job Market Research
- Job Search Strategy
- Interview Preparation Guides
- Job Application Support
- Consultation Calls
- Ongoing Support

## Individual Services
Individual services are available, but the chatbot should first explain that most clients choose packages because they provide a more complete job-search toolkit.

Individual service pricing:
- Resume/CV Writing: AUD 400 onwards
- Cover Letter Writing: AUD 150 onwards
- LinkedIn Optimization: AUD 350 onwards

## Resume/CV Writing
DreamShift creates editable Word documents tailored to the client’s target roles, job market, skills, experience, and career direction. The writing process uses existing documents, questionnaires, job market research, and ATS keyword research.

## Cover Letter Writing
Cover letters are created to match the client’s target roles and CV versions. Advanced and Ultimate packages include 3–4 cover letter versions.

## LinkedIn Optimization
DreamShift focuses on both LinkedIn optimization and branding.

Optimization includes:
- Headline optimization
- Skill and keyword alignment
- Top skills section guidance
- Experience section bullet points
- Project section guidance
- Recruiter-friendly profile structure

Branding may include:
- LinkedIn banner design
- Featured section images
- Personal brand visuals

## ATS Keyword Research
DreamShift analyzes relevant job titles and job descriptions to identify:
- Similar job titles
- Areas of expertise
- Soft skills
- Technical skills
- Qualifications
- Recruiter expectations

## Interview Preparation Guides
Advanced and Ultimate packages include interview preparation guides for each interview. These are guides, not a full interview coaching guarantee. DreamShift does not guarantee interview performance or job offers.
