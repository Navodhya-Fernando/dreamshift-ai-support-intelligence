# DreamShift Critical Business Facts

## Packages and Prices

DreamShift has three main packages.

Essential Package:
- Price: AUD 750
- Ideal for someone with one specific job opening
- Includes Resume/CV Writing: 1 version
- Includes Cover Letter Writing: 1 version
- Includes Basic LinkedIn Optimization
- Includes ATS Keyword Research
- Includes 2 consultation calls
- Includes 1 month ongoing support
- Revisions: unlimited within 1 week

Advanced Package:
- Price: AUD 800
- Ideal for someone who wants experts to handle every document and guide them
- Includes Resume/CV Writing: 3–4 versions
- Includes Cover Letter Writing: 3–4 versions
- Includes Advanced LinkedIn Optimization
- Includes LinkedIn Banner + Free Designs
- Includes ATS Keyword Research
- Includes unlimited consultation calls
- Includes Job Search Strategy: 50+ jobs in 10 hours
- Includes Interview Preparation Guide for each interview
- Includes ongoing support until the client lands a job
- Revisions: unlimited within 1 month
- Does not include the same 2-month job application support as Ultimate

Ultimate Career Package:
- Price: AUD 1500
- Ideal for someone who wants DreamShift to apply for jobs and handle everything
- Includes Resume/CV Writing: 3–4 versions
- Includes Cover Letter Writing: 3–4 versions
- Includes Advanced LinkedIn Optimization
- Includes LinkedIn Banner + Free Designs
- Includes ATS Keyword Research
- Includes unlimited consultation calls
- Includes Job Search Strategy: 50+ jobs in 10 hours
- Includes Interview Preparation Guide for each interview
- Includes Job Application Support for 2 months
- Includes a Dedicated Senior Writer
- Includes ongoing support until the client lands a job
- Revisions: unlimited within 1 month

## Individual Services

Individual services are available, but the chatbot should recommend packages first.

Individual service prices:
- Resume/CV Writing: AUD 400 onwards
- Cover Letter Writing: AUD 150 onwards
- LinkedIn Optimization: AUD 350 onwards

## Instalments

Clients do not have to pay everything at once.
Instalments are available with an additional AUD 10 charge.
The exact payment arrangement can be discussed during the free consultation call.

## Guarantee

DreamShift guarantees interviews, not job offers.
If the client does not land at least one job interview within 60 days of receiving finalized documents, and they meet the guarantee conditions, they may request either:
- 50% refund
- Complete rewrite of CV, Cover Letter, and LinkedIn at no additional cost

## Urgent Delivery

Do not promise urgent delivery.
Urgent delivery depends on team availability.
Ask the user to contact DreamShift or book a free consultation.

## Job Application Support

If the user wants DreamShift to apply for jobs, recommend the Ultimate Career Package.
Essential and Advanced do not include the same 2-month job application support as Ultimate.

## Never Promise

Never promise:
- A job
- A job offer
- Hiring
- Visa outcomes
- PR outcomes
- Interview performance
- Urgent delivery without team confirmation
