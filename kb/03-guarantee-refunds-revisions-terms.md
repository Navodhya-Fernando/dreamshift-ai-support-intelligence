# DreamShift Guarantee, Refunds, Revisions & Terms

## 60-Day Interview Guarantee
DreamShift guarantees that the client’s DreamShift-optimized Resume/CV, Cover Letter, and LinkedIn will help them land at least one job interview within 60 days of receiving finalized documents, subject to the guarantee conditions.

If the client does not land at least one job interview within 60 days of receiving finalized documents, they may request one of the following:
- A 50% refund of the service fee
- A complete rewrite of all documents: CV, Cover Letter, and LinkedIn at no additional cost

## When the Guarantee Period Starts
The 60-day guarantee period begins from the date finalized documents are delivered to the client.

## Conditions for the Guarantee
To qualify for the 60-day guarantee, all of the following must apply:
- The client must have actively applied to relevant job roles using the documents DreamShift provided during the 60-day period.
- The roles applied for must be relevant to the client’s field, experience level, and visa eligibility.
- The claim must be submitted within 75 days of receiving finalized documents.

## Refund Claim Window
The client must submit the claim within 75 days of receiving finalized documents.

## Non-Refundable Services
The following are not covered by the refund policy:
- Fully completed and approved services where the 75-day claim window has passed.
- Revisions requested beyond the original agreed scope of work.
- Express or expedited delivery services.
- Services where the client did not actively apply to relevant roles during the 60-day period.

## Refund Process
To submit a claim:
1. Contact admin@dreamshift.net.
2. DreamShift will review and respond within 3–5 business days.
3. If approved, refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 4–7 business days.

## Important Guarantee Clarification
DreamShift guarantees interviews, not jobs.

The chatbot must make this clear:
- DreamShift does not guarantee job offers.
- DreamShift does not guarantee hiring decisions.
- DreamShift does not guarantee interview performance.
- DreamShift provides interview preparation guides, but does not promise the client will pass interviews.
